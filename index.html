About

Swiss Designs is a brand identity for student graphic designer Dario Amato. He is currently living in Canada, where he is pursuing his goal to spread the power of art through graphic design.

The name SWISS is an abbreviation for Simple Work In Simple Software, which identifies Dario's workflow and designs as he takes simple concepts and elevates the work.
